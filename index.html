Recharge dates

The recharge date sets the billed month on the invoice (e.g. 18 May → RECHARGE MAY 2026). Package Expiry is always the same calendar day in the next month (e.g. 12 May → 12 June). Pick the recharge date from the calendar; the old expiry is not used.
